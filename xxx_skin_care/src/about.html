Vegetable Glycerin
  • Moisturizes the skin by drawing water from the air into the skin's outer layer.
  • Improves skin barrier function, making it smoother and softer. Can help alleviate dryness and irritation.
Honey
  • Natural humectant, meaning it attracts and retains moisture, keeping skin hydrated.
  • Contains antibacterial properties, which can be beneficial for acne-prone skin.
  • Rich in antioxidants, it helps to combat free radicals and promote healthy skin aging.
Black Pepper
  • Contains antioxidant properties that can help protect the skin from oxidative stress.
  • Stimulates blood circulation when used topically, promoting a healthy, glowing complexion.
  • May have antibacterial properties, potentially beneficial for acne-prone skin.
Sugar
  • Acts as a gentle exfoliant, sloughing off dead skin cells and promoting cell turnover.
  • Helps to unclog pores and prevent acne.
  • Leaves the skin feeling smoother and softer.
Sea Salt
  • Exfoliates dead skin cells, promoting smoother and brighter skin.
  • Helps to cleanse pores and reduce inflammation.
  • Contains minerals such as magnesium and potassium, which can nourish the skin.
Brown Sugar
  • Similar to white sugar but contains molasses, which adds moisture and nutrients to the skin.
  • Acts as an exfoliant, revealing fresh, glowing skin.
  • Can help improve the skin's texture and appearance over time.
Cinnamon
  • Has antimicrobial properties that can help combat acne-causing bacteria. Increases blood flow to the skin, promoting a healthy, radiant complexion. Contains antioxidants that protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals.
Lemon
  • Contains vitamin C, which brightens the skin and promotes collagen production. Natural astringent properties help to tighten pores and reduce excess oil. Acts as a mild exfoliant due to its citric acid content.
Blueberries
  • Rich in antioxidants, particularly vitamin C and anthocyanins, which protect the skin from oxidative stress and premature aging. Helps to improve skin texture and tone. Contains vitamins and minerals that nourish and hydrate the skin, promoting a youthful appearance.
